The Kraken

Simple data collection and analysis in rural areas without internet

Log In Learn More

Model your Data

Design customized forms for data collection according to your needs. Share these forms with the members of your organization.

Collect data offline

Use a laptop, tablet or smartphone to fill out forms and collect data. Once loaded in the browser, theKraken runs without any internet connection.

Synchronize and Analyze

If internet access is available, synchronize your data among the members of your organization. Run analysis in the app or export to use your own tool.

Crafted with

The Kraken was built by Kate, Khadija, John and Patrick at Fullstack Academy.

We used Angular and Node for the App, PouchDB and CouchDB for offline storage and synchronization and Service Workers for full offline capabilities.
